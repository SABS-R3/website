How to apply

We encourage applications from students with a wide range of academic backgrounds, including Mathematics, Computing, Statistics, Chemistry, Biochemistry, Physics, Biology, Pharmacology and Engineering. Fully funded studentships for home and EU students are now available. Before submitting a full application for a position on the programme, all candidates are required to send a copy of their most recent CV via e-mail to the administrator at dtcenquiries@dtc.ox.ac.uk.

Application Procedure

Application information and selection criteria can be found on the central University pages here.

Before submitting a full application for a position on the programme, all candidates are required to send a copy of their most recent CV via e-mail to the Academic Administrator at dtcenquiries@dtc.ox.ac.uk to assess your suitability for the course. If advised to submit an application, all applicants are required to follow the University guidelines on the admissions process.

Applications for entry in 2019-20 are now open and the next deadline is 25 January 2019.

The fastest, most efficient and preferred method of application is via the secure online application form. In exceptional circumstances, a paper application form can be requested from Graduate Admissions. The code for the EPSRC and MRC Systems Approaches to Biomedical Science CDT programme of study is R5_1.

In order to apply for a Studentship (whether Research Council or industry funded) and place on the programme, it is essential that candidates enter the correct Studentship Code on the application form.

This is: 19SABSCDT

Non-UK candidates are encouraged to explore alternative funding options as well; there are a number of university scholarship opportunities you can be considered for separately if you apply to the programme by 25 January 2019, please see http://www.ox.ac.uk/admissions/graduate/fees-and-funding for details.

Please note that the University has imposed an application fee, details of which are found in the Application Guide. This fee is payable for each application made, unless you are also applying for associated courses listed under Multiple Applications here. The application fee is non-refundable regardless of the outcome of the application.

Funding

Studentships are available for students enrolled at the University of Oxford funded by the EPSRC and MRC, University of Oxford, industrial partners and charitable trusts. Restrictions on eligibility set by individual funding bodies will apply. Information on eligibility requirements for EPSRC-MRC studentships can be found here. In order to apply for a Studentship (whether Research Council or industry funded) please enter the following Studentship code on the course application form: 19SABSCDT.

Non-UK candidates are eligible for industrial funding held by the CDT which will be awarded competitively. This would cover full fees and a stipend for living expenses, and candidates will be considered automatically based on their application to the programme. You are encouraged to explore alternative funding options as well; there are a number of university scholarship opportunities you can be considered for separately if you apply to the programme by 25 January 2019, please see here for details.
