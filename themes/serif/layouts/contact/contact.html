How to find us

The Rex Richards Building is located off South Parks Road, behind the Physical and Theoretical Chemistry Laboratory. When you get to the entrance on the north west side (marked on the map below) please use the buzzer to ask to for admission to the building. See also the Map of Oxford University Science Area , where the Rex Richards Building is marked as number 4, or the Searchable Map of all the Oxford University buildings.

Parking

Unfortunately there is no parking available at the building. The nearest car parking on Mansfield Road (metered parking). Click on the marker on the map for driving directions. View University of Oxford DTC: Directions in a larger map.
