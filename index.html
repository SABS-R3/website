SABS:R3

The new EPSRC CDT in Sustainable Approaches to Biomedical Science: Responsible and Reproducible Research (SABS:R3), will train students in cutting-edge systems approaches to biomedical research and, uniquely within the UK, in advanced practices in software engineering. Our renewed goal is to bring about a transformation of the research culture in computational biomedical science.
